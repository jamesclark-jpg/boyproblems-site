Boy Problems are an independent disco-rock band from Naarm/Melbourne. Drawing from a wide range of influences including indie rock, disco and house, Boy Problems embrace experimentation and are driven to never be boring. Boy Problems have been exciting audiences across Melbourne and throw a party not to be missed.

Boy Problems is the project of long time friends Cass Willcocks (she/her) and James Clark (he/him). A shared love of music, dancing and generally having a good time have long been the cornerstones of their friendship, but it wasn't until 2022 that they finally decided to start writing and producing their own music in a makeshift backyard studio.

They launched onto the Melbourne music scene in the summer of 2022 with a series of raucous house party sets. Their energetic performance and memorable songs fired up dance floors and immediately set tongues wagging.
