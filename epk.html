Biography

Boy Problems launched on the Naarm/Melbourne music scene in the summer of 2022 and quickly built a reputation for high energy shows that get crowds moving.

Boy Problems began when long time friends Cass Willcocks (she/her) and James Clark (he/him) decided to shake off the lockdown years by creating music together. Working independently out of a makeshift garage studio, they are committed to doing things their own way. Drawing on a range of influences from rock, pop and dance music they make big loud music for big loud feelings.

Embracing experimentation, Boy Problems are anything but boring. Seamlessly blending big riffs, house beats and memorable hooks to create music that is full of energy and personality.

Following the success of their first two singles, Boy Problems is taking on a residency at iconic Melbourne music venue, The Old Bar. The band will be bringing their high energy show to the stage every Wednesday between October 11-25.

Music

Singles

Boy Problems self-released two singles in 2023 after a summer of raucous house party shows left audiences wanting music they can take home. Lift Me Up was featured by Music Victoria on Spotify and Cool Cool Girl was played on community radio across the country.
